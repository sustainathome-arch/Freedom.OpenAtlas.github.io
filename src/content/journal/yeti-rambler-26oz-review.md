This bottle has become part of our daily routine. Each of us owns one and uses it every day. We've moved away from plastic bottles and to-go cups almost entirely, sticking mainly to water, and this has been our go-to way to stay hydrated. During the summer we load it up with ice and water in the morning and still have cold, refreshing drinks well into the day.

What really stands out is the durability. These bottles have taken plenty of abuse — dropped on pavement, bounced across jagged volcanic rock, even slipped into rivers like the McKenzie. One was briefly lost along the Rogue Gorge and still came back in solid shape. Even after all that, the exterior coating has held up with only minor cosmetic wear.

The chug cap is convenient and easy to use, though it's not perfect. It's something to keep an eye on — having a backup cap on hand is a smart move if you're using it heavily outdoors.
